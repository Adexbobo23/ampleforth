Get Ampl

AMPL is a price-stable but supply volatile cryptocurrency that targets the CPI-adjusted dollar. It is used as a unit of account and collateral asset. See the documentation to learn more about the AMPL protocol.

 How to Get AMPL

AMPL can be purchased on the following exchanges:

  1. Cowswap (Recommended)
  2. Uniswap V2
  3. KuCoin
  4. Bitfinex

AMPL Contract Address:

  • 0xd46ba6d942050d489dbd938a2c909a5d5039a161

 Wrapped AMPL

Holding Wrapped AMPL (wAMPL) is equivalent to holding raw AMPL in terms of exposure to network growth. However only raw AMPL can be used for administrative actions like tranching, redeeming, minting, and rotating. Users can unwrap and re-wrap at: https://wrap.ampleforth.org/

Wrapped AMPL (wAMPL) Contract Address:

  • 0xEDB171C18cE90B633DB442f2A6F72874093b49Ef
